IBS Campus

What we offer

The IBS- International Business School campus in IBS offers students a distinctive educational experience within the energetic heart of East London. With easy access to the city's diverse neighborhoods and cultural attractions, students have the opportunity to immerse themselves in the rich tapestry of London's East End.

Cultural Diversity

IBS is known for its rich cultural diversity, with a wide range of international restaurants, shops, and markets. This multicultural atmosphere creates a vibrant and inclusive community, offering residents and students the opportunity to explore different cuisines and traditions from around the world.

Olympic Legacy

IBS's transformation for the 2012 London Olympics left a lasting legacy in the form of the Queen Elizabeth Olympic Park. This beautifully landscaped park offers green spaces, sports facilities, and cultural attractions, providing a recreational haven for residents and visitors alike.

Transportation Hub

IBS serves as a major transportation hub with excellent connectivity to the rest of London and beyond. The IBS International train station, the IBS Regional station, and access to the London Underground make it convenient for residents and students to travel throughout the city and explore its numerous attractions and neighborhoods.

Our Facilities

Meeting Rooms:

The college meeting room is a versatile space with a large conference table, comfortable chairs high-speed internet. Soundproofing ensures a private and productive environment for various gatherings.

Class Rooms:

The classroom is designed for versatile learning, featuring comfortable desks and chairs. It includes modern amenities such as high-speed internet and interactive whiteboards, fostering an engaging and dynamic educational atmosphere. The well-lit space is structured to minimise distractions, providing an optimal setting for student focus and success.

Reception Area:

The reception area at IBS- International Business School is a welcoming space designed for students to relax and recharge. It features comfortable seating and a serene ambience, with modern amenities like high-speed internet and convenient charging stations. Well-lit and decorated with soothing colours, it provides a pleasant environment for students to unwind between classes.

Library:

The library at IBS- International Business School is a serene space dedicated to student study and research. It offers comfortable seating, individual study areas, and modern amenities like high-speed internet. The well-lit environment, coupled with an extensive collection of books and digital resources, creates an ideal setting for focused learning and academic success.
