Employability

and Careers Services

Empowering your Career Journey

Support for Future Study Options

If you're considering further education, our Careers Team can provide guidance on potential study options that align with your career goals. We'll help you explore undergraduate programs, certifications, and other learning opportunities that can enhance your qualifications.

Short Professional Appointments

A short guidance appointment with us offers clear, professional advice. It’s an ideal starting point for addressing your initial queries with one of our Career Practitioners and exploring your options.

Foster Your Future

During your time with us, you’ll also work on your “Career Passport”. This tool is designed to help you record and showcase the employability skills you develop, providing you with a valuable bank of evidence for job applications and interviews.

Get in touch

Whether you're a current student, an alumnus, or someone interested in learning more about our employability and careers services, we're here to assist you.

Reach out to us for enquiries, appointment bookings, and general information. Our team is dedicated to responding promptly to your emails.
